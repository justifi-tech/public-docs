# Refund a Terminal Payment

## Via API
We offer 2 different APIs to execute a refund.
Via Refund Payment API: You need to payment ID from the checkout record and create a refund as documented here: https://developer.justifi.ai/tag/Refunds#operation/CreateRefund.

Via Checkout API: Use this to do a general refund of the checkout as documented here: https://docs.justifi.tech/api-spec#tag/Checkouts/operation/RefundCheckout 

## Via JustiFi Dashboard
In addition you can refund a payment on the JustiFi dashboard.


To refund the payment you need the payment ID. 
Go to https://app.justifi.ai/ and choose “Payments” from the “Payments” dropdown; filter the payment list by the payment ID; click into the payment detail view and click the “Refund” button.


To refund the checkout you need the checkout ID. 
Go https://app.justifi.ai/ and choose “Checkouts” from the “Payments” dropdown; on the checkouts list click into any checkout and replace the last part of the url with your checkout ID and click enter; on the detail view of your checkout click the “Refund” button.
